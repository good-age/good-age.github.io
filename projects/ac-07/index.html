Extending the self.

To the creator, to the visionary, the tool is an extension of the self. An instrument exposes the purity of our intent; it is the door through which our ideas must pass to manifest in the world.

A human-first approach to computing requires us to dig deeper, to build a tool worthy of our minds; it requires an interface that can synchronize our devices to the natural 0's and 1's of the brain.

We hope you join us for the journey.
